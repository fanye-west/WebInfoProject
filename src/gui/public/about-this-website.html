ABOUT US

We have developed this website to help people self-manage their diabetes. As a patient, you can record and store your health data, which can be remotely monitored by your clinician. This enables clinicians to track the health of their patients, ensuring they stay on track and maintain good health.

To access these features, you must sign up and log in.
